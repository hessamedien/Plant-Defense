HOW TO PLAY

Goal: Defend your lawn from zombies using plants!

Sun: Collect falling sun to get resources for plants.

Plants:

  • Sunflower: Generates sun over time
  • Peashooter: Shoots peas at zombies
  • Wall-nut: High health defensive plant
  • Cherry Bomb: Explodes, damaging multiple zombies
  • Snow Pea: Slows down zombies

Strategy: Place plants strategically to stop zombies from reaching your house!
